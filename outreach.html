FIRST Team 2500 Herobotics

FIRST Team 2500 Herobotics is an urban robotics team from Patrick Henry High School in Minneapolis, MN. Herobotics seeks to increase inclusion and diversity in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics. Herobotics has advanced to the FIRST Championship eight times over the past eleven years through dedicated work to inspire youth with the message that everyone can succeed in science and technology.
